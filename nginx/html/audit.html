What is an AI Automation Audit?

An AI Automation Audit is not about tools. It is about understanding how your business actually works today — and where automation can replace manual effort, reduce errors and save time.

We analyze your real workflows, decision points and integrations, and turn complexity into a clear, actionable automation plan.

How the audit works

1. Review

We map your current workflows, tools and bottlenecks. No assumptions — only real processes.

2. Identify

We detect automation opportunities with the highest ROI, including AI and non-AI optimizations.

3. Recommend

You receive a clear automation plan: what to automate, how, and in what order.

What you get

  • Clear map of your current workflows
  • List of automation opportunities ranked by impact
  • Recommended tools and integrations
  • AI vs non-AI justification (where AI makes sense — and where it doesn’t)
  • Next steps roadmap
